I'm Tom. I'm an Engineering Lead.
I like to work in:
  • a) businesses that understand the importance of Tech
  • b) highly-collaborative, high-trust teams doing Trunk-Based Development
  • c) cross-functional teams, where every role is a hat to be worn rather than a gate to be kept
  • d) teams doing continuous delivery, deploying to production in <5 mins with great observability
  • e) teams using functional, statically typed languages and an IntelliJ IDE, practising continuous refactoring
I enjoy hard technical challenges and their intersection with Product-thinking, Systems-thinking and broader business and management strategy. Great things happens when teams are trusted and empowered to build good quality products, experiment and learn. The individual contributor model of ownership breaks down quickly for any moderately hard problem and businesses should go after problems that are hard to solve.
